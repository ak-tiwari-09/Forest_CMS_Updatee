Platform Features

Everything you need to manage
forest complaints efficiently

📝

Easy Complaint Filing

Report illegal logging, poaching, encroachment, or forest fires with personal details, category selection, supporting documents, and identity proof — all in one streamlined form.

📡

Real-Time Tracking

Track your forest complaint status live using your unique reference ID. Know exactly where your case stands at every step.

✅

Complaint Verification

Every complaint is reviewed and verified by authorized forest officials before action is initiated, ensuring authenticity and reducing false reports.

🛡️

Secure Document Upload

Attach identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID) and supporting evidence such as photos of encroachment or damage securely. Your data is encrypted and protected.

🔔

Status Notifications

Forests receive real-time status updates whenever their forest complaint progresses, keeping them informed every step of the way.

How Forest Complaint Management System Works

From registration to resolution — a simple, transparent 5-step process.

1

Register Complaint

Fill in your personal details, select the forest issue category, describe the incident and upload supporting documents.

→
2

Get Reference ID

Receive a unique FC Reference ID instantly. Use this to track your complaint at any time.

→
3

Forest Officer Review

Our forest Office reviews your complaint, assigns priority, and begins working on the resolution.

4

Our forest department reviews your complaint, assigns priority, and begins working on the resolution.

→
